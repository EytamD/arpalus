Product Recognition

Product Recognition is a Computer Vision technology that identifies exact products in images or video, usually at SKU level.

As part of a larger system, it replaces manual product checks with scalable, reliable product-level monitoring - helping businesses understand what is present, missing, misplaced, or incorrectly handled.
