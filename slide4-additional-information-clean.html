Slide 4 — Diagnosis

Additional Information

Before deciding on next steps, I would request more information to understand whether errors come from data, labels, model behavior, or real-world conditions.

1

Product Images

Check if products are visually distinct or generic with similar packaging and limited visual cues.

2

Confusion Matrix

See exactly which SKUs are confused, and whether errors concentrate in specific product pairs.

3

Labeling Review

Verify training images were labeled correctly. Inconsistent SKU labels cause systematic errors.

4

Not-Identified Breakdown

Is it poor image quality, missing catalog items, or low confidence?

5

Store Environment

Check whether lighting, angle, blur, or crowded shelves affected results.

6

Confidence Scores

Did mistakes happen with high or low confidence? Decide when manual review is needed.

7

Training Data per SKU

How many images exist per SKU, and are rare products under-represented vs. popular ones?
