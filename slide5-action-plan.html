Action Plan

With budget for one major improvement initiative, I would focus on the largest error source: same-brand SKU confusion.

What I Would Choose

I would target and re-label the data for visually similar products from the same brand.

  • Collect more real field images for the confused product pairs.
  • Review and correct labeling quality for those SKUs.
  • Add stronger reference images for each product.
  • Build a focused training set around the products the model confuses.

Why This Initiative?

Same-brand confusion = 18% of all cases / 46% of all errors

The model seems to identify the brand, but not distinguish well enough between SKUs inside that brand.

This is likely addressable through better labeling, more targeted images, and stronger reference data.

Because this is the largest error bucket, solving it should significantly reduce the total error rate.

How I Would Measure Success

Correctly identified ↑Measure an increase in correct SKU-level recognition.
Same-brand confusion ↓Measure a drop in products identified as another product from the same brand.
Confused SKUs ↑Measure per-SKU recall on the product pairs that caused most errors.
Human review ↓Measure fewer cases that require manual validation or correction.
