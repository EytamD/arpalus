Slide 1 — Problem & Business Need

Product Recognition

Product Recognition is a Computer Vision technology that identifies exact products in images or video, usually at SKU level.

✕ Problem

A camera can capture products, but software cannot reliably know what exact item it is seeing without barcode scans, manual entry, or human review.

✓ Solution

Product Recognition turns visual input into SKU-level product data — enabling stock checks, checkout validation, shrink detection, and other workflows.
