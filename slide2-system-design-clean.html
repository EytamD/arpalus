Slide 2 — System Design

From Image to Action

The system should be designed around the business workflow, not only around the recognition model.

Discovery & Scope

Start With The Use Case

Define the industry, existing solutions, customer pain, target users, and financial value.

  • What problem are we solving?
  • Who is the expected customer and user?
  • Where can accurate recognition create business value?
  • Example: OOS, planogram, availability.
Data Needed

Required Inputs

  • Visual data — product & field images, angles, lighting, packaging versions.
  • Product data — SKU, name, brand, barcode, size, package, category.
  • Training / validation — marked product locations, verified SKU labels, similar-product examples.
  • Business data — planogram, inventory, prices, promotions.

Build to Deploy

Operational Steps

  • Define use case → collect & label data.
  • Choose / adapt model → fine-tune & test.
  • Add human review for validation and correction.
  • Integrate business systems and deploy MVP.
  • Monitor errors and update data continuously.
