Initial Conclusions
Accuracy is the main issueWith 39% errors or unresolved cases, the model would create operational noise and reduce trust.
Fine-grained recognition is weakThe largest error bucket is same-brand confusion, suggesting difficulty telling similar SKUs apart.
Data quality should be investigatedI'd check labeling quality, field image coverage, catalog completeness, and whether training data reflects real conditions.
